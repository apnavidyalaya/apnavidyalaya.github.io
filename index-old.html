Why choose us?

Why us?

Every year, several lakhs of IT graduates come out of engineering colleges in India. But, the jobs are limited.

Of the people that graduate every year, a significant percentage of students are not properly skilled to get a job. Although Indian students are respected all across the world for their smartness, their lack of training and skills land them nowhere. When these students don't get job/internship after initial struggle, they loose self confidence. In India, especially Greater Noida, we have so much of colleges that the seats in these colleges remain vacant. They advertise to get more students. But, we are seriously lacking when it comes to providing our students with some training and orientation for placement.

While interviewing candidates at UrbanCLap, I myself have realised that candidates are generally smart, but they lack skills. So, I have decided to come forward and start Apna Vidyalaya to provide proper IT training to students so that they are well suited for placements.
